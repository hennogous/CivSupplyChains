# Civ Supply Chains

**CSC** is a Civilization VI overhaul mod that adds a supply chain system to the game. Cities can grow Quarters — district-like structures tied to specific trade goods and production chains — unlocking unique buildings, specialist slots, and trade bonuses as they develop.
