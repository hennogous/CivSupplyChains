## 3. Brewery or Winery

The Brewery and Winery are mutually exclusive buildings, but have the exact same benefits, so you can choose which one fits the materials surrounding your Brewers' Quarter the best.

* Unlocks at Guilds
* Requires a Malthouse in the Quarter
* Costs 160 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/7/74/Civ6Production.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084523' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Production

* +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/7/74/Civ6Production.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084523' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Production from the local Malthouse
* +0.1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/2/2a/Civ6Culture.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084510' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Culture and +0.1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market
* +0.1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/8/82/Civ6Faith.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084513' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Faith and +0.1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Worship building
   * With Medieval Faires, Worship buildings provides +10% <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold in the city for each adjacent Brewery or Winery
