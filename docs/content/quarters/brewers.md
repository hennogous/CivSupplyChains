# Brewers' Quarter

![Brewers' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Brewers'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| **Base materials** | Bananas, Grapes (Wine), Maize, Rice, Wheat | Barley (R2), Potato (LAR), Sorghum (R2) |
| **Specialty materials** | Citrus, Cocoa, Coffee, Honey, Spices, Sugar | Peat (JNRW), Plums (CR), Pomegranates (CR) |

- **Main yield focus:** 🪙 Gold
- **Unlocks at:** Craftsmanship
- **Base cost:** 60 🔨 Production
- **Maintenance cost:** 1 🪙 Gold

---

## Adjacency Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +2 🪙 Gold from each adjacent City Center, and +1 🍎 Food and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Commercial Hub, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Holy Site, and +1 🙏 Faith in return
- **City Lights:**
  - +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
  - +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🔨 Production if the Quarter is adjacent to a river

---

## Stage 1: Materials

- Base materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Malthouse
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation
- Specialty materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Distillery
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation

---

## Stage 2: Malthouse

- **Unlocks at:** Currency
- **Cost:** 80 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +1 🍎 Food to each adjacent Granary, and receive +1 🪙 Gold in return
  - With Feudalism, a Granary adjacent to a Malthouse provides +10% growth in the city
- +1 🪙 Gold from the local Brewery or Winery, and Distillery
- +1 🪙 Gold bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes

---

## Stage 3: Brewery or Winery

The Brewery and Winery are mutually exclusive buildings with identical benefits. Choose whichever fits the materials surrounding your Brewers' Quarter.

- **Unlocks at:** Guilds
- **Requires:** A Malthouse in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 160 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Malthouse
- +0.1 🎭 Culture and +0.1 🪙 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market
- +0.1 🙏 Faith and +0.1 🪙 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Worship building
  - With Medieval Faires, Worship buildings provide +10% 🪙 Gold in the city for each adjacent Brewery or Winery
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +3 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🪙 Gold bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in the city

---

## Stage 4: Distillery

- **Unlocks at:** Metal Casting
- **Requires:** A Malthouse in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 250 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Malthouse
- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +2 🪙 Gold for every 5 Citizens in the city for each adjacent Palace, Big Ben, Casa de Contratación, Colossus, Great Lighthouse, Great Zimbabwe and Torre de Belém
- **With Cartography:**
  - Unlocks the **Create Spirits** project in the city
  - +1 Product slot for Spirits products to the Palace, Big Ben, Casa de Contratación, Colossus, Great Lighthouse, Great Zimbabwe and Torre de Belém
  - Spirits products grant +4 🪙 Gold to international trade routes to and from the city, increased to +8 if the Palace or Wonder is adjacent to a Distillery
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +3 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🪙 Gold bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
