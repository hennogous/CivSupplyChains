# Apothecaries' Quarter

![Apothecaries' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Apothecaries'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| **Base materials** | Cocoa, Coffee, Honey, Incense, Olives, Spices, Tea, Tobacco | Algae (R2), Aloe (R2), Cannabis (CH), Coca (LAR), Coral (R2/SO), Hemp (CH), Kelp (SO), Medicinal Herbs (R2), Mushrooms (R2), Poppies (R2), Sandalwood (R2), Seashells (R2), Sponge (R2), Yerba Mate (LAR) |
| **Specialty materials** | Copper, Iron, Mercury, Silver | Gold (R2/SR) |

- **Main yield focus:** 🔬 Science
- **Unlocks at:** Craftsmanship
- **Base cost:** 60 🔨 Production
- **Maintenance cost:** 1 🪙 Gold

---

## Adjacency Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Holy Site, and +1 🙏 Faith in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Commercial Hub, Encampment and Campus, and +1 🔬 Science in return
- **City Lights:**
  - +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 🔬 Science in return
  - +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 🔬 Science in return
- +1 🔨 Production if the Quarter is built adjacent to Forest or Rainforest

---

## Stage 1: Materials

- Base materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Herbalist
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation
- Specialty materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Alchemist
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation

---

## Stage 2: Herbalist

- **Unlocks at:** Irrigation
- **Cost:** 80 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +1 🔬 Science, with a -1 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 🙏 Faith to each adjacent Shrine, and receive +1 🪙 Gold in return
  - With Mysticism, Shrines provide +10% 🙏 Faith in the city for each adjacent Herbalist
- +1 🪙 Gold from the local Apothecary and Alchemist
- +1 🙏 Faith bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes

---

## Stage 3: Apothecary

- **Unlocks at:** Guilds
- **Requires:** A Herbalist in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 160 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Herbalist
- +2 🔬 Science, with a -2 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +0.1 🔬 Science and +0.1 🪙 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market and Armory
  - With Gunpowder, friendly units in the city territory heal +10 HP per turn for each Apothecary adjacent to the Armory
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +2 🔬 Science and +1 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🔬 Science bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in the city

---

## Stage 4: Alchemist

- **Unlocks at:** The Enlightenment
- **Requires:** A Herbalist in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 250 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Herbalist
- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +3 🔬 Science, with a -3 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 🔬 Science and +1 🪙 Gold for every 5 Citizens in the city for each adjacent University
- **With Scientific Theory:**
  - Unlocks the **Create Pseudoscience** project in the city
  - +1 Product slot for Pseudoscience products to all Universities in the empire
  - Pseudoscience products grant +10% 🔬 Science in the city, increased to +20% if the University is adjacent to an Alchemist
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +1 🔬 Science and +2 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🔬 Science bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
