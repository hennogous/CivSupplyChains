# 4. Stonemasons' Quarter

![Stonemasons' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Stonemasons'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| Base materials | Hills, Stone | Granite (R2), Limestone (R2), Travertine (R2) |
| Specialty materials | Gypsum, Marble | Alabaster (R2), Obsidian (SR), Quartz (RL) |

* Main yield focus: Production
* Unlocks at Craftsmanship
* Base cost: 60 Production

---

* +1 Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain
* +1 Gold from each adjacent Government Plaza, Commercial Hub and City Center, and +1 Production in return
* +1 Faith from each adjacent Renaissance Era (or earlier) Wonder
* City Lights:
   * +1 Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 Production in return
   * +1 Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 Production in return
* +1 Production if the Quarter is built on Hills terrain

## 1. Materials

* Base materials improvements: +1 Gold from each adjacent Stonecutter
   * +2 Gold to an Industry, and +3 Gold to a Corporation
* Specialty materials improvements: +1 Gold from each adjacent Stonemason
   * +2 Gold to an Industry, and +3 Gold to a Corporation

## 2. Stonecutter

* Unlocks at Masonry
* Costs 80 Production

* +1 Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
   * +2 Production from an Industry, and +3 Production from a Corporation
* +1 Production, with a -1 Gold maintenance cost
* +1 Production to any adjacent Tier 1 Government Building, and receive +1 Gold in return
   * With Defensive Tactics, each Stonecutter adjacent to a Tier 1 Government Building provides +10% Production to the construction of Ancient, Medieval and Renaissance Walls in cities within 6 tiles
* +1 Gold from the local Stonemason and Sculptor's Studio
* +1 Production bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 Gold in return from international routes

## 3. Stonemason

* Unlocks at Guilds
* Requires a Stonecutter in the Quarter
* Costs 160 Production

* +1 Production from the local Stonecutter
* +2 Production, with a -2 Gold maintenance cost
* +0.1 Production and +0.1 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market
* +0.1 Production and +0.1 Faith per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Renaissance (or earlier) Wonder
   * With Reformed Church, +20% Production to the construction of any Renaissance Era (or earlier) Wonder for each adjacent Stonemason
* +1 Citizen slot, and +2 Production and +1 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
* +1 Production bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 Gold in return from international routes
* +1 Amenity

## 4. Sculptor's Studio

* Unlocks at Humanism
* Requires a Stonecutter in the Quarter
* Costs 250 Production

* +1 Production from the local Stonecutter
* +1 Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
   * +2 Production from an Industry, and +3 Production from a Corporation
* +3 Production, with a -3 Gold maintenance cost
* +1 Production and +1 Gold for every 5 Citizens in the city for an adjacent Monument
   * With Nationalism:
   * Unlocks the Create Statue project in the city
   * +1 Product slot for Statue products to all Monuments in the empire
   * Statue products grant +5 Loyalty in the city, increased to +10 if the Monument is adjacent to a Sculptor's Studio
* +1 Citizen slot, and +1 Production and +2 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
* +1 Production bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 Gold in return from international routes
* +1 Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
