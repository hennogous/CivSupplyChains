# Blacksmiths' Quarter

![Blacksmiths' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Blacksmiths'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| **Base materials** | Copper, Hills, Iron | Lead (R2), Tin (R2) |
| **Specialty materials** | Aluminium, Silver | Gold (R2/SR), Platinum (R2) |

- **Main yield focus:** 🔨 Production
- **Unlocks at:** Craftsmanship
- **Base cost:** 60 🔨 Production
- **Maintenance cost:** 1 🪙 Gold

---

## Adjacency Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain
- +2 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Encampment, and +2 🔨 Production in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Commercial Hub and Industrial Zone, and +1 🔨 Production in return
- **City Lights:**
  - +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 🔨 Production in return
  - +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 🔨 Production in return
- +1 🔨 Production if the Quarter is built on Hills terrain

---

## Stage 1: Materials

- Base materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Smelter
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation
- Specialty materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Foundry
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation

---

## Stage 2: Smelter

- **Unlocks at:** Bronze Working
- **Cost:** 80 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +1 🔨 Production, with a -1 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 🔨 Production to any adjacent Barracks or Stable, and receive +1 🪙 Gold in return
  - With Apprenticeship, Barracks and Stables provide +10% 🔨 Production to the training of melee units in the city for each adjacent Smelter
- +1 🪙 Gold from the local Blacksmith and Foundry
- +1 🔨 Production bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes

---

## Stage 3: Blacksmith

- **Unlocks at:** Apprenticeship
- **Requires:** A Smelter in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 160 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Smelter
- +2 🔨 Production, with a -2 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +0.1 🔨 Production and +0.1 🪙 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market and Armory
  - With Military Engineering, +1 free promotion to melee and anti-cavalry units trained in the city for each Blacksmith adjacent to the Armory
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +2 🔨 Production and +1 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🔨 Production bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity

---

## Stage 4: Foundry

- **Unlocks at:** Metal Casting
- **Requires:** A Smelter in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 250 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Smelter
- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +3 🔨 Production, with a -3 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 🔨 Production and +1 🪙 Gold for every 5 Citizens in the city for each adjacent Factory
- **With Steel:**
  - Unlocks the **Create Advanced Gearing** project in the city
  - +1 Product slot for Advanced Gearing products to all Factories in the empire
  - Advanced Gearing products grant +10% 🔨 Production in the city, increased to +20% if the Factory is adjacent to a Foundry
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +1 🔨 Production and +2 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🔨 Production bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
