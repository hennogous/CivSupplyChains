# Goldsmiths' Quarter

![Goldsmiths' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Goldsmiths'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| **Base materials** | Amber, Silver | Alabaster (R2), Coral (R2/SO), Gold (SR), Obsidian (SR), Quartz (R2), Seashells (R2) |
| **Specialty materials** | Diamonds, Jade, Pearls | Lapislazuli (R2), Opal (CR), Ruby (R2) |

- **Main yield focus:** 🪙 Gold
- **Unlocks at:** Craftsmanship
- **Base cost:** 60 🔨 Production
- **Maintenance cost:** 1 🪙 Gold

---

## Adjacency Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Holy Site, and +1 🙏 Faith in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Commercial Hub, Encampment and City Center, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- **City Lights:**
  - +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
  - +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🔨 Production if the Quarter is adjacent to a river

---

## Stage 1: Materials

- Base materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Gem & Metalworks
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation
- Specialty materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Jeweler
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation

---

## Stage 2: Gem & Metalworks

- **Unlocks at:** Currency
- **Cost:** 80 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +1 🙏 Faith to each adjacent Shrine, and receive +1 🪙 Gold in return
  - With Mysticism, Shrines provide +10% 🙏 Faith in the city for each adjacent Gem & Metalworks
- +1 🪙 Gold from the local Goldsmith and Jeweler
- +1 🪙 Gold bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes

---

## Stage 3: Goldsmith

- **Unlocks at:** Apprenticeship
- **Requires:** A Gem & Metalworks in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 160 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Gem & Metalworks
- +0.2 🪙 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market and Armory
  - With Mercenaries, purchasing military units is 20% cheaper in the city for each Goldsmith adjacent to the Armory
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +3 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🪙 Gold bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in the city

---

## Stage 4: Jeweler

- **Unlocks at:** Humanism
- **Requires:** A Gem & Metalworks in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 250 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Gem & Metalworks
- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +2 🪙 Gold for every 5 Citizens in the city for each adjacent Palace, Big Ben, Casa de Contratación, Colossus, Great Lighthouse, Great Zimbabwe and Torre de Belém
- **With Mercantilism:**
  - Unlocks the **Create Crown Jewels** project in the city
  - +1 Product slot for Crown Jewels products to the Palace, Big Ben, Casa de Contratación, Colossus, Great Lighthouse, Great Zimbabwe and Torre de Belém
  - Crown Jewels products grant +10% 🪙 Gold in the city, increased to +20% if the Palace or Wonder is adjacent to a Jeweler
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +3 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🪙 Gold bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
