| [[goldsmiths\|Goldsmiths' Quarter]] | 🪙 Gold |
| [[brewers\|Brewers' Quarter]] | 🪙 Gold |

All Quarters:
- Unlock at **Craftsmanship**
- Base cost: **60 🔨 Production**
- Maintenance cost: **1 🪙 Gold**
- One per city, counts toward district slots
- Each subsequent Quarter in the same city costs more

## Materials

Many resources from the standard game (including Rise & Fall, Gathering Storm and the New Frontier Pass) have been mapped as either base materials or specialty materials for use in supply chains. Resources directly linked to animals are marked in italics, and can optionally be included.

A number of resources from popular resource mods have also been mapped to supply chains, with a setting to hide all other resources from these mods if you only want the mapped resources.

**Compatible mods:**
- CIVITAS Resources (CR) by Sailor Cat, thecrazyscotsman, SeelingCat, chimp and p0kiehl
- Latin American Resources (LAR) by Leugi
- Resourceful 2 (R2) by Amatheria, p0kiehl and Zegangani
- Sukritact's Oceans (SO) by Sukritact
- Sukritact's Resources (SR) by Sukritact
- Wetlands (JNRW) by JNR

See [[../reference/materials|Materials Table]] for the full resource mapping.
