# 2. Tailors' Quarter

![Tailors' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Tailors'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| Base materials | Cotton, Sheep | Bamboo (R2), Flax (CSC), Hemp (CH), Llamas (LAR) |
| Specialty materials | Dyes, Silk, Silver | Cashmere (R2), {{gold}} Gold (R2 / SR) |

* Main yield focus: {{culture}} Culture
* Unlocks at Craftsmanship
* Base cost: 60 {{production}} Production

---

* +1 {{production}} Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain, and +1 {{gold}} Gold in return
* +1 {{gold}} Gold from each adjacent Harbor, and +1 {{production}} Production in return
* +1 {{gold}} Gold from each adjacent Commercial Hub, Diplomatic Quarter and Theater Square, and +1 {{culture}} Culture in return
* City Lights:
   * +1 {{production}} Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 {{culture}} Culture in return
   * +1 {{gold}} Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 {{culture}} Culture in return
* +1 {{production}} Production if the Quarter has access to fresh water

## 1. Materials

* Base materials improvements: +1 {{gold}} Gold from each adjacent Textile Workshop
   * +2 {{gold}} Gold to an Industry, and +3 {{gold}} Gold to a Corporation
* Specialty materials improvements: +1 {{gold}} Gold from each adjacent Fashion House
   * +2 {{gold}} Gold to an Industry, and +3 {{gold}} Gold to a Corporation

## 2. Textile Workshop

* Unlocks at Construction
* Costs 80 {{production}} Production

* +1 {{production}} Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
   * +2 {{production}} Production from an Industry, and +3 {{production}} Production from a Corporation
* +1 {{culture}} Culture, with a -1 {{gold}} Gold maintenance cost
* +1 {{production}} Production to each adjacent Lighthouse, and receive +1 {{gold}} Gold in return
   * With Naval Tradition, Lighthouses provide +20% {{production}} Production in the city to Renaissance Era (or earlier) naval units for each adjacent Textile Workshop
* +1 {{gold}} Gold from the local Tailor and Fashion House
* +1 {{production}} Production bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 {{gold}} Gold in return from international routes

## 3. Tailor

* Unlocks at Guilds
* Requires a Textile Workshop in the Quarter
* Costs 160 {{production}} Production

* +1 {{production}} Production from the local Textile Workshop
* +2 {{culture}} Culture, with a -2 {{gold}} Gold maintenance cost
* +0.1 {{culture}} Culture and +0.1 {{gold}} Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market and Chancery
   * With The Enlightenment, the Chancery provides +3 Influence points for each adjacent Tailor
* +1 Citizen slot, and +2 {{culture}} Culture and +1 {{gold}} Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
* +1 {{culture}} Culture bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 {{gold}} Gold in return from international routes
* +1 {{amenity}} Amenity in the city

## 4. Fashion House

* Unlocks at Humanism
* Requires a Textile Workshop in the Quarter
* Costs 250 {{production}} Production

* +1 {{production}} Production from the local Textile Workshop
* +1 {{production}} Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
   * +2 {{production}} Production from an Industry, and +3 {{production}} Production from a Corporation
* +3 {{culture}} Culture, with a -3 {{gold}} Gold maintenance cost
* +1 {{culture}} Culture and +1 {{gold}} Gold for every 5 Citizens in the city for each adjacent Amphitheater, Bolshoi Theatre, Broadway and Sydney Opera House
   * With Opera and Ballet:
   * Unlocks the Create Haute Couture project in the city
   * +1 Product slot for Haute Couture products to all Amphiteaters, Bolshoi Theatre, Broadway or Sydney Opera House in the empire
   * Haute Couture products grant +10% {{culture}} Culture in the city, increased to +20% if the Amphiteater or Wonder is adjacent to a Fashion House
* +1 Citizen slot, and +1 {{culture}} Culture and +2 {{gold}} Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
* +1 {{culture}} Culture bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 {{gold}} Gold in return from international routes
* +1 {{amenity}} Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
