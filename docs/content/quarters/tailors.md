# Tailors' Quarter

![Tailors' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Tailors'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| **Base materials** | *Cotton*, *Sheep* | Bamboo (R2), Flax (CSC), Hemp (CH), *Llamas* (LAR) |
| **Specialty materials** | Dyes, Silk, Silver | Cashmere (R2), Gold (R2/SR) |

- **Main yield focus:** 🎭 Culture
- **Unlocks at:** Craftsmanship
- **Base cost:** 60 🔨 Production
- **Maintenance cost:** 1 🪙 Gold

---

## Adjacency Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Harbor, and +1 🔨 Production in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Commercial Hub, Diplomatic Quarter and Theater Square, and +1 🎭 Culture in return
- **City Lights:**
  - +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 🎭 Culture in return
  - +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 🎭 Culture in return
- +1 🔨 Production if the Quarter has access to fresh water

---

## Stage 1: Materials

- Base materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Textile Workshop
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation
- Specialty materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Fashion House
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation

---

## Stage 2: Textile Workshop

- **Unlocks at:** Construction
- **Cost:** 80 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +1 🎭 Culture, with a -1 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 🔨 Production to each adjacent Lighthouse, and receive +1 🪙 Gold in return
  - With Naval Tradition, Lighthouses provide +20% 🔨 Production in the city to Renaissance Era (or earlier) naval units for each adjacent Textile Workshop
- +1 🪙 Gold from the local Tailor and Fashion House
- +1 🔨 Production bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes

---

## Stage 3: Tailor

- **Unlocks at:** Guilds
- **Requires:** A Textile Workshop in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 160 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Textile Workshop
- +2 🎭 Culture, with a -2 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +0.1 🎭 Culture and +0.1 🪙 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market and Chancery
  - With The Enlightenment, the Chancery provides +3 🌐 Influence points for each adjacent Tailor
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +2 🎭 Culture and +1 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🎭 Culture bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in the city

---

## Stage 4: Fashion House

- **Unlocks at:** Humanism
- **Requires:** A Textile Workshop in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 250 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Textile Workshop
- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +3 🎭 Culture, with a -3 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 🎭 Culture and +1 🪙 Gold for every 5 Citizens in the city for each adjacent Amphitheater, Bolshoi Theatre, Broadway and Sydney Opera House
- **With Opera and Ballet:**
  - Unlocks the **Create Haute Couture** project in the city
  - +1 Product slot for Haute Couture products to all Amphitheaters, Bolshoi Theatre, Broadway or Sydney Opera House in the empire
  - Haute Couture products grant +10% 🎭 Culture in the city, increased to +20% if the Amphitheater or Wonder is adjacent to a Fashion House
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +1 🎭 Culture and +2 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🎭 Culture bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
