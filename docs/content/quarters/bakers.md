# Bakers' Quarter

![Bakers' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Bakers'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| **Base materials** | Bananas, *Cattle*, Maize, Rice, Wheat | Barley (R2), Flax (CSC), Quinoa (LAR), Sorghum (R2) |
| **Specialty materials** | Cocoa, Coffee, Grapes (Wine), Honey, Olives, Salt, Spices, Sugar, Tea | Berries (R2), Dates (R2), Poppies (R2), Saffron (R2), Strawberry (R2), Sugar Maple (R2), Tomato (R2) |

- **Main yield focus:** 🍎 Food
- **Unlocks at:** Craftsmanship
- **Base cost:** 60 🔨 Production
- **Maintenance cost:** 1 🪙 Gold

---

## Adjacency Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent City Center and Commercial Hub, and +1 🍎 Food in return
- +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Entertainment Complex, Water Park and Garden district (Leugi), and +1 🎭 Culture in return
- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent Brewers' Quarter, and +1 🪙 Gold in return
- +1 🔨 Production from every 2 adjacent river segments
- **City Lights:**
  - +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 🍎 Food in return
  - +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 🍎 Food in return

---

## Stage 1: Materials

- Base materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Water Mill or Wind Mill
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, and +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation
- Specialty materials improvements: +1 🪙 Gold from each adjacent Café
  - M&C: +2 🪙 Gold to an Industry, and +3 🪙 Gold to a Corporation

---

## Stage 2: Water Mill or Wind Mill

The Water Mill has moved from the City Center to the Bakers' Quarter, and is only available if the quarter has an adjacent river. The Wind Mill has the exact same yields and bonuses, and is only available when the quarter has no adjacent river.

- **Unlocks at:** The Wheel
- **Cost:** 80 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +1 🍎 Food, with a -1 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 🪙 Gold from the local Bakery and Café
- +1 🍎 Food bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🍎 Food to an adjacent Granary, and receive +1 🪙 Gold in return
- **At Feudalism**, a Water Mill or Wind Mill adjacent to an improved base materials resource unlocks:
  - A **Town Crier** can be established in the adjacent City Center, if it has a Granary
  - The Town Crier provides the city with +3 Loyalty and +1 🌐 Influence point per turn

---

## Stage 3: Bakery

- **Unlocks at:** Guilds
- **Requires:** A Water Mill or Wind Mill in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 160 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Flour Mill
- +2 🍎 Food, with a -2 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and +2 🍎 Food and +1 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🍎 Food bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in the city
- +0.2 🍎 Food and +0.2 🪙 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market
- **At Medieval Faires**, a Bakery adjacent to an improved base materials resource unlocks:
  - An **Innkeeper** can be established in an adjacent Commercial Hub with a Market
  - The Innkeeper provides the city with +2 🏠 Housing, +1 👤 Citizen slot and +1 Great Merchant point per turn

---

## Stage 4: Café

- **Unlocks at:** The Enlightenment
- **Requires:** A Water Mill or Wind Mill in the Quarter
- **Cost:** 250 🔨 Production

### Yields & Bonuses

- +1 🔨 Production from the local Flour Mill
- +1 🔨 Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
  - M&C: +2 🔨 from an Industry, +3 🔨 from a Corporation
- +3 🍎 Food, with a -3 🪙 Gold maintenance cost
- +1 👤 Citizen slot, and an additional +1 🍎 Food and +2 🪙 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
- +1 🍎 Food bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 🪙 Gold in return from international routes
- +1 🎭 Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
- +1 🎭 Culture for every 5 Citizens in the city to each adjacent Zoo, Ferris Wheel or Conservatory (Leugi's Garden District), and +1 🪙 Gold to the Café in return
- **At Urbanization**, a Café adjacent to improved base and specialty materials resources unlocks:
  - A **Groundskeeper** can be established in an adjacent Entertainment Complex with a Zoo
  - A **Ride Technician** in a Water Park with a Ferris Wheel
  - A **Horticulturist** in a Garden district with a Conservatory (Leugi)
  - All three services provide the city with +2 🗺️ Tourism, +1 👤 Citizen slot and +1 Great Person point per turn
  - **With Monopolies & Corporations:**
    - Unlocks the **Commission Fine Pastries** project in cities with a Café, producing a Fine Pastries product
    - Fine Pastries can be placed in an Aristocrat's private collection or traded, granting +2 🏠 Housing
