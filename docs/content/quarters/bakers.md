## 2. Water Mill or Wind Mill

The Water Mill has moved from the City Center to the Bakers' Quarter, and is only available if the quarter has an adjacent river. The Wind Mill has the exact same yields and bonuses, and is only available when the quarter has no adjacent river.

* Unlocks at Wheel
* Costs 80 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/7/74/Civ6Production.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084523' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Production

* +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/7/74/Civ6Production.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084523' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
   * +2 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/7/74/Civ6Production.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084523' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Production from an Industry, and +3 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/7/74/Civ6Production.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084523' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Production from a Corporation
* +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/6/65/Civ6Food.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084516' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Food, with a -1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold maintenance cost
* +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold from the local Bakery and Café
* +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/6/65/Civ6Food.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084516' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Food bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold in return from international routes
* +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/6/65/Civ6Food.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084516' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Food to an adjacent Granary, and receive +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold in return
* At Feudalism, a Water Mill or Wind Mill adjacent to an improved base materials resource unlocks:
   * A Town Crier can be established in the adjacent City Center, if it has a Granary.
   * The Town Crier provides the city with +3 Loyalty and +1 Influence point per turn.

   * With Monopolies & Corporations:

      * Fine Pastries can be placed in the private collection of an Aristocrat or traded with other players, and grant +2 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/a/ae/Housing6.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200428064015' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Housing.
