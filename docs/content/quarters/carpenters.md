## 1. Materials

Until Machinery and Mercantilism are unlocked, unimproved Woods and Rainforest tiles, respectively, serve as base material sources and receive the <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold bonuses below. This is because Lumber Mills become available relatively late in the tech tree, with Rainforest placement unlocked even later.

* Base materials improvements: +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold from each adjacent Joinery
   * +2 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold to an Industry, and +3 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold to a Corporation
* Specialty materials improvements: +1 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold from each adjacent Luthier
   * +2 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold to an Industry, and +3 <img src='https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/civilization/images/b/bc/Civ6Gold.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/18?cb=20200501084519' width='18' style='vertical-align:middle'/> Gold to a Corporation
