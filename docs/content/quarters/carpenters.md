# 5. Carpenters' Quarter

![Carpenters' Quarter](../images/Civ%20Modding%20Projects%20-%20Carpenters'%20Quarter.jpg)

| | Standard game | Mods |
|---|---|---|
| Base materials | Copper, Iron, Rainforest, Woods | Bamboo (R2), Cherry Tree (R2), Oak (R2), Pine (R2), Rubber (R2) |
| Specialty materials | Aluminium, Amber, Dyes, Silk, Silver | Ebony (R2), Sandalwood (R2), Sugar Maple (R2) |

* Main yield focus: Production
* Unlocks at Craftsmanship
* Base cost: 60 Production

---

* +1 Production from each adjacent base or specialty materials resource from this supply chain, and +1 Gold in return
* +1 Gold from each adjacent Harbor, Commercial Hub and Encampment, and +1 Production in return
* +1 Gold from each adjacent Theater Square, and +1 Culture in return
* City Lights:
   * +1 Production from each adjacent Rural Community, and +1 Production in return
   * +1 Gold from each adjacent Urban Borough, and +1 Production in return
* +1 Production if the Quarter has access to fresh water

## 1. Materials

Until Machinery and Mercantilism are unlocked, unimproved Woods and Rainforest tiles, respectively, serve as base material sources and receive the Gold bonuses below. This is because Lumber Mills become available relatively late in the tech tree, with Rainforest placement unlocked even later.

* Base materials improvements: +1 Gold from each adjacent Joinery
   * +2 Gold to an Industry, and +3 Gold to a Corporation
* Specialty materials improvements: +1 Gold from each adjacent Luthier
   * +2 Gold to an Industry, and +3 Gold to a Corporation

## 2. Joinery

* Unlocks at Bronze Working
* Costs 80 Production

* Until Machinery is unlocked, +1 Production from each adjacent Woods tile
* Until Mercantilism is unlocked, +1 Production from each adjacent Rainforest tile
* +1 Production from each adjacent base materials improvement
   * +2 Production from an Industry, and +3 Production from a Corporation
* +1 Production, with a -1 Gold maintenance cost
* +1 Production to each adjacent Lighthouse, and receive +1 Gold in return
   * With Shipbuilding, Lighthouses provide +20% Production in the city to Renaissance Era (or earlier) naval units for each adjacent Joinery
* +1 Gold from the local Carpenter and Luthier
* +1 Production bonus to trade routes to the city, and +1 Gold in return from international routes

## 3. Carpenter

* Unlocks at Construction
* Requires a Joinery in the Quarter
* Costs 160 Production

* +1 Production from the local Joinery
* +2 Production, with a -2 Gold maintenance cost
* +0.1 Production and +0.1 Gold per Citizen to the city for each adjacent Market, and Barracks or Stable
   * With Engineering, Barracks and Stables provide +20% Production in the city to Medieval Era (or earlier) siege units for each adjacent Carpenter
* +1 Citizen slot, and +2 Production and +1 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
* +1 Production bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +1 Gold in return from international routes
* +1 Amenity in the city

## 4. Luthier

* Unlocks at Humanism
* Requires a Joinery in the Quarter
* Costs 250 Production

* +1 Production from the local Joinery
* +1 Production from each adjacent specialty materials improvement
   * +2 Production from an Industry, and +3 Production from a Corporation
* +3 Production, with a -3 Gold maintenance cost
* +1 Culture and +1 Gold for every 5 Citizens in the city for each adjacent Amphitheater, Bolshoi Theatre, Broadway and Sydney Opera House
   * With Opera and Ballet:
   * Unlocks the Create String Instruments project in the city
   * +1 Product slot for String Instruments products to all Amphiteaters, Bolshoi Theatre, Broadway or Sydney Opera House in the empire
   * String Instruments products grant +10% Tourism in the city, increased to +20% if the Amphiteater, Bolshoi Theatre, Broadway or Sydney Opera House is adjacent to a Luthier
* +1 Citizen slot, and +1 Production and +2 Gold to Citizens in the Quarter
* +1 Culture bonus to all trade routes to the city, and +2 Gold in return from international routes
* +1 Amenity in all cities within 6 tiles
